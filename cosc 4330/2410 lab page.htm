Turn In Regulation

For the programming assignment:
  • Electronic submission
    •  Under your bayou account, type the following command:

      ~cela/public/bin/submit assignment# file1 file2 ...
       

    • For example, if you need to submit assignment #0, you should type:

      ~cela/public/bin/submit 0 file1 file2 file3
       

General Grading Regulation

  • The determination of submission time is based on the time shown on your submission tree.
    • The deadline will be the 24:00 of each due day.
    • Late assignments will be assessed a penalty of 5 points per day unless announced otherwise. You will be given a total of three grace days to be used at your discretion.
  • The programs will be compiled and tested on the bayou.uh.edu.
  • I should use cxx to compile if you submit only one .c file. Otherwise, you need to  provide your own makefile.
  • To be fair, I will grade only once based on the running of your programs.
  • Besides the correctness, the style of coding, formatting and documentation also contribute to your grade.

How To Check Your Score:

  •  Under your bayou account, type the following command:
     

    ~cela/public/bin/score asgn#
     

    • For example, for assignment #2, you should type
       

      ~cela/public/bin/score 2
